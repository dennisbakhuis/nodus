# Technology list

The list view shows the same data as the radar in a sortable, filterable
table. It's the fastest way to scan many technologies, compare attributes
side by side, or export a subset of the catalogue.

## Filtering

The sidebar exposes the same filters as the radar:

- **Segment**, **Ring**, **Movement** — multi-select chips.
- **Search** — free-text matching name, description, and tags.
- **Strategic relevance**, **Min TRL**, **Time to mainstream** — quantitative
  filters.
- **Status** — choose between _On Radar_, _Backlog_, and _Archive_.
- **Has factsheet** / **Has peer references** — narrow to topics with
  attached collateral.

Filters are mirrored in the URL — copy the link to share a filtered view.

## Sorting and columns

Click a column header to sort. Hold _Shift_ and click a second header to
sort by multiple columns.

## Bulk actions

Tick rows to select them. The toolbar shows actions available for the
selection — typically _Export selected_.

## Adding a new technology

Writers see a **+ Add** button in the top bar. Adding from the list lets you
fill out all required metadata before placing the topic on a ring.
