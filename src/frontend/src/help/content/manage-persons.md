# Persons

Persons are people referenced as owners, contributors, or experts on
individual technologies.

## Adding a person

Click **New person** and fill in at least the full name. Company, email,
role, department, and notes are optional but help when filtering or when
reaching out.

## Why have a persons registry?

Centralising people means:

- A consistent display name across topics (no typos).
- Filtering the list view by _Owner is X_.
- Generating contact lists per segment or ring for a cycle review.

## Deactivating

Persons cannot currently be deleted from the UI to preserve historical
attribution. Remove them from individual topics if they should no longer
appear.
