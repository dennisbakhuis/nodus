# Manage

The manage area is where writers and admins configure the radar:

- **Cycles** — create, close, and theme assessment cycles.
- **Settings** — organisation name, logo, contact details.
- **Segments** — the angular slices of the radar and their themes.
- **Persons** — people referenced as owners or contributors.
- **Users** — application accounts and their roles.
- **Visibility** — control which fields each role can see.
- **Backup** — export and restore the full database.
- **Import** — bring in peer references from another instance.
- **API access** — manage API keys for programmatic access.

Pick a section from the sidebar and the help button will update with
detailed guidance for that area.
